Lasagna

Description

Follow this recipe to create the most delicious lasagna for your family and friends!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Two cans of tomato sauce
  • Two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses (parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta cheese)

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna (meat, cheese, lasagna noodles)
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
